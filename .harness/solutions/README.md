## Purpose

.harness/solutions stores reusable fixes and recovery playbooks discovered during
harness work.

## Authority

Solutions are secondary-context until referenced by instructions, validators, or
active execution-input artifacts.

## Use

Prefer refreshing a high-overlap solution over duplicating one. Include symptoms,
root cause, exact fix, validation, and when not to apply it.
