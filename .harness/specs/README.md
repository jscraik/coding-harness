## Table of Contents

## Purpose

.harness/specs holds source-owned specs that define desired behavior.

## Authority

Specs are execution-input authority when admitted by the active artifact index.
Research and strategy files can inform specs, but cannot replace them.

## Use

Specs should include scope, non-goals, acceptance evidence, and links to tracker
or local-only ownership.
