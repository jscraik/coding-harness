## Purpose

.harness/decisions records accepted tradeoffs and durable architecture choices.

## Authority

Decision records are decision authority. They explain why a path was chosen and
what would be required to revisit it.

## Use

Record the decision, alternatives considered, evidence, consequences, and review
date. Do not bury permanent constraints in transient implementation notes.
